ABOUT

Welcome to the KHMIS Portal. This portal will be a gateway to wealth of information regarding the health indicators of Kerala. The information available on this portal is derived data from data uploaded by the districts. KHMIS data specifically designed to support planning, management, and decision making based on on Grading of facilities, various indicators at Block, District at State as well as National Level.
